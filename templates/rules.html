Rules & Gameplay

All Javascript

Questions & solutions all use Javascript as the de facto language. We may eventually add support for other languages.

No DOM / Outside Access

All solutions have no access to a DOM, the file-system, the Internet, etc. You've got the full Javascript language at your disposal.

Ten Questions

Each race will have 5 randomly chosen questions. Questions can be added by anyone (but must be valid & have a valid solution).

No Time Limit

You have as much time as you need to finish the race, but be warned the other racers may go faster than you, which leads to...

Modifiers!

Get a question right fast enough & watch the other competitors suffer on the next question as one of the modifiers takes effect:

  • Normal (no gameplay modification)
  • No recursion (only regular loops allowed)
  • No loops (only recursion & brute force)
  • No conditionals
  • One-liner! (only one statement allowed)

Modifiers are only in place for one question, after which they are cleared for the subsequent question.
